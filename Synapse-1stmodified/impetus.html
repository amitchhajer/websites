Impetus

Update: Problem statement up

"Impetus" rds set over the years in terms of competition as well as immaculate judging, Concepts is now one of the most awaited technical events. The highlight of Concepts continues to be the Project Exhibition cum Competition which is open to final year engineering students all over . This draws the best of the talent from the engineering colleges over the country. The projects are judged by experts and academicians from the IT industry. This exhibition has had unparalleled success in promoting student-industry interaction. Other than the widely attended project exhibition cum competition, it has a range of workshops, seminars, programming contests, paper presentation competitions, circuit designing and machine making competitions.

Event Structure

There will be two phases:

  • Prelim round: The participants would be required to submit their abstract which will be selected on the basis of-----

    The participants would be required to submit the solution to the problem statement in written form by 25th Mar 2010.

    The co-coordinators will read the abstract and approve the idea that is to be worked upon. This is basically done so that a group can know that they are proceeding in the right direction. Teams would be allowed to participate in the final event only if their abstracts are found satisfactory (according to the rules given). The results would be declared before 29th Mar. 2010.

  • Final round: The selected participants are expected to prepare a working model for their abstract. The final round will be on 30th Mar 2010. Each team would be given a desk to showcase their models on the final day. All teams would explain their respective models to the judges verbally.

Rules

  • Students from any department/year can participate.
  • The team should not consist of more than 4 members and not less than 2 members.
  • Inputs and outputs should have the same representations in the working model.
  • Every team would have to submit a description of their model to the coordinators, before actually implementing the idea. They would also have to provide their team name, member’s names, group leader name, model name, institution, resources and materials used.
  • Any extra Resources like water supply, metal etc. or something that is really needed in advance would be provided only when notified well in advance.
