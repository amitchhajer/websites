Welcome to Impetus and concepts

INC is a celebration -- a celebration of culture, technology and life. These three aspects dominate the human mind and the world today. INC is all about commemoration of these three in an electrifying atmosphere. Its not just about a college fest , it is a celebration and a declaration of liberation. INC represents the defiance and the freedom that the name PICT carries with it. In those three days, INC manages to generate pure exuberance and challenge to master minds of the country all the while giving an experience of a lifetime.

INC brings with it a bigger and better. PICT has always thought its students to carve their own path and not just follow convention .Fearlessness is what resonates within the walls of this campus and fearlessness is what INC is all about. No boundaries , no worldly compulsions . Its all about bringing to the world what makes you special . In our day to day life we hardly find the time to be who we really are. INC is all about being yourself . And its not just the entertainment or the culture that enriches synapse but the very pull of the challenges that tempt the most brilliant of the brains in the country that makes INC what it is and that is legendary.

INC is synonymous to youthfulness. And the youth brings together an entirely different world , a world where your art and your own brilliance or just your ability to have fun makes you special. From brilliant minds with computing skills that could lower the smartest of computers and with grace that represents the real essence of performing arts synapse embodies.

INC is the place to be if you really want to experience what celebration is all about.
